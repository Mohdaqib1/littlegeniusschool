Our Mission and Our Vision

We at Little Genius School, endeavour to provide a curriculum with a difference. One that balances excellence in learning with an all round cultural, physical, mental and moral development. Our attempt is to make the most formative and beautiful years of our students lives a joy forever. We encourage our children to think independently, to respect themselves and others and to value excellence.

Popular Classes

Classes for Your Kids

Creating a Solid Foundation for Future Learning ( Playgroup )

Our Playgroup offers creative, engaging and age-appropriate play-based activities in a specially designed learning space. The programme is designed to spark a child’s imagination, creativity and curiosity while building their confidence and self-management skills. Teachers focus on creating a ‘safe space’ (physically and emotionally) for our youngest learners to spark their natural curiosity, nurture confidence, and develop positive attitudes toward school.

Personalised Attention and Learning ( Pre School and Pre Primary )

We recognise and value that your child’s emotional and social well-being is shaped from a very early age. We also know that your child is most likely to become a confident learner when placed in an environment in which they feel secure to express themselves without fear of judgement. We strive to establish a safe and nurturing environment within our Kindergarten classes (Nursery – KG), where your child can feel safe and supported. Our dedicated full-time teacher and teaching partner make it a priority to build a strong connection with your child, enabling them to understand their unique qualities and adapt activities accordingly. These specially designed activities are aimed at fostering your child’s strengths and helping them thrive.

Developing Child as An Inquirer ( Classes l to lll )

The Primary Years Programme creates a stimulating and challenging environment that nurtures a child’s natural curiosity, creativity and ability to reflect, and helps them to develop a lifelong love of learning. Whether for 1st or 3rd grade aged students, we carefully support your children to gradually take greater responsibility and ownership for their own learning through our inquiry-led framework.

Forming Students into Creative , Critical and Reflective Thinkers ( Classes IV – V )

These Years are a unique time in your child’s development. This is a period of tremendous intellectual, physical and emotional growth. That’s why our curriculum is broad and balanced and widens students’ knowledge, understanding and experiences. It also ensures that academic excellence flourishes alongside arts, music and sport. We emphasise conceptual understanding and encourage students to make links between their learning and the world in which they live.
