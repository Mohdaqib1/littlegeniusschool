Learn About Us

Best School For Your Kids

Little Genius School was founded in 2015 with the goal of creating a school that values academic excellence, character development, and community engagement. Today, we have a team of highly qualified and experienced teachers who are dedicated to providing each child with an individualized learning experience. Our facilities include spacious classrooms, a well-equipped library, a computer lab, and a playground. We follow a rigorous academic curriculum that prepares children for success in school and beyond.

  • Comprehensive educational program for nursery to class 5 students.
  • Highly qualified and experienced teachers committed to individualized learning.
  • State-of-the-art facilities, including spacious classrooms, a well-equipped library, a computer lab, and a playground.
